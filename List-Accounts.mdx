---
title: "List Accounts"
description: "List Accounts lists the child accounts within the parent account."
api: GET https://cloud.cdata.com/api/poweredby/account/list
contentType: application/json
---


## Responses

### 200

`HTTP 200 OK`

<Expandable title="Response body">

<ResponseField name="accounts" type="array of objects" required>
  <ResponseField name="id" type="string" required>
    The internal Connect AI account Id.
  </ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="externalId" type="string" required>
    The Embedded Cloud external account identifier.
  </ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="created" type="string" required>
    The time this account was created.
  </ResponseField>
</ResponseField>

</Expandable>
