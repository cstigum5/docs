---

Follow these steps to connect your API:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type `API` in the search field, then click the datasource name.
</Step>
<Step>
Connect and configure your API. Three settings tabs are available. Start with the global settings, then continue to create tables and set permissions. Click the links below to view detailed documentation.
	- [Global Settings](/APIGlobalSettings)—set global connection properties, authentication, headers, and pagination.
	- [Tables](/APICreateTable)—create tables, configure properties and filter columns.
	- [Permissions](/APIPermissions)—manage user access.
</Step>
</Steps>
