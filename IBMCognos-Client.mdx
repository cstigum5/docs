## Prerequisites

Before you can configure and use IBM Cognos Analytics with Connect AI, you must first connect a data source to your Connect AI account. See [Sources](/Sources) for more information.

You must also generate a Personal Access Token (PAT) on the [Settings](/Settings#access-tokens) page. Copy this down, as it acts as your password during authentication.

## Connect to Connect AI

After you have completed the prerequisites above, follow the steps below:

<Steps>
<Step>
Download the Connect AI JDBC driver from the Integrations page and install it to the `<cognos>\drivers` directory.
</Step>
<Step>
Open IBM Cognos Analytics and navigate to **Manage** > **Data server connections**.
</Step>
<Step>
Click the icon to add a data server.
</Step>
<Step>
Select **Connect AI**.
</Step>
<Step>
In the connection editor, enter the JDBC URL to the Connect AI server. See the [IBM Cognos Analytics documentation](https://www.ibm.com/en/cognos-analytics) for more information.
</Step>
<Step>
In the username field, enter your Connect AI username. This is displayed in the top-right corner of the Connect AI interface. For example, *test@cdata.com*.
</Step>
<Step>
In the password field, enter the PAT you generated on the [Settings](/Settings#access-tokens) page.
</Step>
<Step>
Save the connection definition.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Test** to confirm that the connection succeeds.
</Step>
</Steps>
