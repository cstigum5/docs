---
title: "Stop Job"
description: "Administrators, impersonating a service user, can use this call to stop the specified job."
api: POST https://cloud.cdata.com/api/job/{jobId}/stop
contentType: application/json
---


### Path Parameters

<ParamField path="jobId" type="string" required>
The Id of the job you want to stop.
</ParamField>

## Responses

### 200

`HTTP 200 OK`
