---
title: "List Connections"
description: "List Connections requests a list of connections for a child account."
api: GET https://cloud.cdata.com/api/poweredby/connection/list
contentType: application/json
---

## Responses

### 200

`HTTP 200 OK`

<Expandable title="Response body">

<ResponseField name="connections" type="array of objects" required>
  <ResponseField name="id" type="string" required>
    The internal Connect AI account Id.
  </ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="name" type="string" required>
    The catalog/connection name.
  </ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="dataSource" type="string" required>
    The data source name. Example: Snowflake, Salesforce, etc.
  </ResponseField>
  <ResponseField name="lastModified" type="string" required>
    The time when the connection was last modified.
  </ResponseField>
</ResponseField>

</Expandable>
