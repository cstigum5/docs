## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect SAP Concur to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *SAP Concur* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Create a custom OAuth application in SAP Concur.
	- You may need to contact your Partner Enablement Manager or Partner Account Manager for SAP Concur for this step.
	- Once the application is created, copy down your clientId and clientSecret.
</Step>
<Step>
In Connect AI, select a region.
</Step>
<Step>
Choose an **Auth Scheme**.
	- If you choose **OAuth**, enter the following information.
		- **OAuth Client Id**—your clientId from the custom OAuth application.
		- **OAuth Client Secret**—your clientSecret from the custom OAuth application.
	- If you choose **OAuthPassword**, enter the following information.
		- **Username**—the SAP Concur username for authentication.
		- **Password**—the user password.
		- **OAuth Client Id**—enter your clientId from the custom OAuth application.
		- **OAuth Client Secret**—enter your clientSecret from the custom OAuth application.
	- If you choose **CompanyLevelAuth**, enter the following information.
		- **CompanyId**—enter the unique identifier for your company.
		- **Company Request Token**—enter your company request token. To obtain one, use the Company Request Token tool, or contact SAP Concur support.
		- **OAuth Client Id**—your clientId from the custom OAuth application.
		- **OAuth Client Secret**—your clientSecret from the custom OAuth application.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Sign in** to connect securely through OAuth. This action opens the SAP Concur sign-in page in a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your SAP Concur account and provide the requested permissions (if applicable).
</Step>
<Step>
At the top of the Connect AI **Add SAP Concur Connection** page, click **Save & Test**.

	- If the connection test succeeds, a **Connection successfully saved** message appears, indicating that your connection has been created. The **Status** on the **Edit Connection** page also changes to **Authenticated**. View the data model of your successful connection in the right pane of the **Edit Connection** page, in the **Data Model** tab.
	- If the connection test fails, ensure that you entered your login information correctly with no stray spaces or other characters. Connect AI displays error messages under the required fields with missing data. Some data sources require that you sign in directly to the source website. If you did not, an error message appears under the **Sign in** button. Correct the errors and try again.
	- Unsuccessful connections are saved as drafts and have a **Status** of **Not Authenticated**. You can return to the connection and authenticate it later.
</Step>
</Steps>
